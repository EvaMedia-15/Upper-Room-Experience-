About Us

We believe in the power of collective prayer and the life-changing impact it has when we stand together in faith. Our gatherings are marked by passionate prayers, lifting our voices and hearts as we pray in the Holy Ghost. We create a space where the Holy Spirit leads, guides, and empowers each session, allowing us to intercede not just for our needs but for those around us, our communities, and the world. Whether you’re new to prayer or have been walking with God for years, our group is a welcoming environment where you can grow in your spiritual journey. Join us nightly at 10 PM everyday on our WhatsApp platform as we tap into the power of prayer, united in the Holy Spirit, and witness the miraculous unfold in our lives.
